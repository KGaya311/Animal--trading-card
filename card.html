Elephant

Elephants are the largest land animals in the world.

  • Scientific name : Elephantidae
  • Average Life Span : 70 years
  • Average weight : 27 to 46 pounds
  • Average height : African elephants stand 3 to 4 m (10–13 ft) and Asian elephants stand 2 to 3.5 m (7–11 ft)
